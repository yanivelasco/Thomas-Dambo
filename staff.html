WHAT WE DO?

We are a creative institution that create experiences all over the world.
Our vision is to make art that inspires people to go explore, have adventures in nature, and demonstrates that trash can be turned into something beautiful.

The Artist

Before embarking on the path of building larger than life sculptures, Thomas lived a diverse life with a multifaceted resume: human beatbox, graffiti/street artist, and rapper to name a few.
Thomas enrolled at the internationally acclaimed Kolding Design School where he earned a master’s degree in interactive design. It was then that he recalled the values his parentsinstilled, using free and recycled materials to build thousands of colorful birdhouses that adorned the streets of Denmark.
His limitless imagination and resourcefulness have created the foundation for his artwork today.

Our Community

It is not easy to create and install art worldwide alone. That being the case, Thomas has created a community in Roskilde, Denmark, where the magic happens.
25 full-time craft professionals work in their eestablishment. A handmade Wearhouse with all the necessary tools to make art out of anything.
